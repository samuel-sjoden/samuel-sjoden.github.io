Project Smokeshow

Engineering Design Project: Aimed to Provide Support to Wildfire Fighters in Response to the 2023 Kelowna Wildfires.

Objective: The project aims to create a mask specifically designed for firefighters working in wildfire conditions, addressing the discomfort and health risks caused by intense smoke inhalation.

Proposal: The mask incorporates interchangeable filters and a snorkel-inspired mouthpiece, focusing on adaptability, comfort, and easy deployment during high-smoke periods. It reduces toxic particle intake while allowing ease of use for firefighters.

Results: A 3D-printed prototype has been developed, and the team aims to further refine the design with higher-quality materials and field testing.

Potential Impact: The mask is designed to improve the safety and working conditions of firefighters, with plans for real-world implementation and future enhancements.
